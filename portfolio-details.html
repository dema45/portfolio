HomeCook

HomeCook is an easy-to-use platform where users can share their recipes through videos and connect with a community of food lovers.It fills the space to easily upload their creations, learn from others, and receive encouragement.

  • Category: App design
  • Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Duration: March 2025 - May 2025
  • Tools: Figma,HTML/CSS

Problem

An individuals constantly search for recipe inspiration and home-cooked meal ideas online. However, most existing platforms focus on either written recipes or video tutorials, offering limited opportunities for users to contribute both. Additionally, there is a lack of interactive features that allow users to share their cooking experiences, give feedback, and form connections within a community. Aspiring and passionate home cooks often need a space to easily upload their creations, learn from others, and receive encouragement. This gap in user-centered, interactive cooking platforms creates a need for a more engaging and community-driven solution.

Project Goal

To create an easy-to-use platform where users can share their recipes through videos and connect with a community of food lovers.

Role

As the UX Lead Designer for the HomeCook platform, my role was to guide the overall user experience design process and ensure that the platform was both intuitive and engaging. I was responsible for understanding user needs and translating them into functional, visually appealing design solutions. I approached the project holistically—balancing usability, accessibility, and aesthetics to create an experience that kept users engaged and satisfied.

Responsibilities

Led the UX design process by conducting user research, creating personas and user journeys, and designing intuitive navigation. I used Figma to build wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes, ran usability tests, and refined the design based on feedback. I developed a consistent visual system and collaborated with developers to ensure accurate implementation. Additionally, I designed community features like comments, likes, and content discovery to enhance user engagement.

Pain Points

Lack of Clear Instructions and Accessibility

Many platforms rely heavily on videos, which are hard to follow for users who prefer written instructions or are in noisy environments. HomeCook solves this by offering both video and detailed step-by-step written recipes to suit more user needs.

Limited Community Interaction and Engagement

Many platforms don’t support interaction, making it hard to ask questions or share feedback. HomeCook encourages engagement through comments and tip-sharing, fostering a more connected and helpful cooking community for all users.

Difficulty in Discovering New Recipes

Most recipe sites lack personalized recommendations or organized navigation. HomeCook solves this with a clean interface and smart browsing tools that help users easily find recipes matching their tastes, diets, and skill levels.

Lack of Trust in Recipe Authenticity and Quality

It’s hard to judge quality on platforms with user-uploaded recipes. HomeCook builds trust through a comment system where users share feedback and results, helping others identify which recipes are reliable and worth trying.

Empathy Map

Persona

Journey Map

To better understand the needs and emotions of users like Sonam—whose goal is to share a homemade pasta recipe video and connect with fellow cooking enthusiasts—I developed a detailed journey map outlining her key actions, emotional states, and improvement opportunities throughout the content creation and engagement flow. This map helped me identify friction points such as anxiety about filming, the lack of support when uploading content, and the need for clearer content creation tools. These insights directly informed the design of HomeCook’s features, including a guided recipe template, split-view video and text interface, and anonymous feedback options to foster confidence, creativity, and connection within the platform.

Takeaway

Working on the HomeCook project deepened my understanding of user-centered design, particularly in addressing the needs of both beginner and experienced home cooks. Through usability studies and iteration, I learned the importance of simplifying navigation, optimizing content layout, and providing visual clarity through an effective color palette. This project helped refine my ability to translate user feedback into meaningful design improvements.

Next Step

Moving forward, I plan to expand HomeCook by incorporating a community-driven event feature where users can host or join cooking sessions. Additionally, I aim to implement a group chat function to foster real-time interaction and knowledge sharing. To ensure accessibility, I also intend to integrate screen reader support and explore ways to make the platform more inclusive for users with disabilities.
